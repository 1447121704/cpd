Enumeration Type Documentation

enum cpd::FgtMethod
strong

Choosing an fgt method.

Enumerator
DirectTree 

Use the direct tree method.

This is the standard transform but with simplification via clustering.

Ifgt 

Use the improved fast gauss transform.

This method does clustering simplifications and a series expansion to reduce the computational complexity, but works poorly with few points or small bandwidths.

Switched 

Switch between DirectTree and Ifgt at a breakpoint.
